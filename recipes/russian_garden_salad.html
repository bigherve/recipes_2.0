Russian Garden Salad

Ingredients

  • 10 romaine lettuce leaves, chopped
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 large cucumber, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Â½ cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Toss the romaine lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, onion, and parsley together in a large bowl; season with salt. Drizzle the lemon juice and olive oil over the salad; stir. Add the sour cream and mix until evenly coated.
